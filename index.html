The Wood School of Music is available to teach your children basic music skills

Our philosphy is based on the idea that being a parent is hard and your offspring have decided to take up a musical instrument which can make it even harder.
We take them off your hands for an hour a week to teach them basic music skills until they get bored and move onto their next obsession.
